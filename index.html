How Are Robots Built? üîß

01

Design & Planning

First, the robot's design is created. Engineers decide what the robot will do, its size, and shape. A 3D model is created using CAD software.

02

Mechanical Structure

The robot's body is built from metal, plastic, or carbon fiber. Joints, gears, and motors are installed to enable movement and functionality.

03

Electronics Assembly

Microcontrollers (Arduino, Raspberry Pi), sensors, motors, and wires are connected. This forms the robot's nervous system that sends signals.

04

Programming

The robot is programmed using Python, C++, or Arduino code. Instructions are written to tell the robot what to do in different situations.

05

Power System

Batteries and power management circuits are installed. Solar panels can also be used for renewable energy sources.

06

Testing & Debugging

The robot is tested, errors are found and fixed. Improvements are made repeatedly until the robot works perfectly.
